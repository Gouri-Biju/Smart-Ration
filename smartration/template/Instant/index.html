Our Story

Delivering smart and transparent ration services since 2024

Smart Ration Portal was born out of the need to bring fairness, accuracy, and digital transparency to the public distribution system. With a unified platform connecting government staff, shopkeepers, and citizens, we aim to simplify ration card management, product distribution, and user communication. Our mission is to empower every household with reliable access to essential commodities — through technology, automation, and trust.

Transparency

We ensure clear, trackable processes from request to delivery — building trust through visible, accountable systems.

Efficiency

Our platform reduces manual effort, automates approvals, and speeds up distribution — saving time for both citizens and officials.

Community

We are committed to reaching every citizen, ensuring no one is left behind in accessing their rightful support.

Growth

With thousands of users onboarded and shops connected, Smart Ration is scaling across regions and raising service standards.
